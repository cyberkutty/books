There are so many changes made by the Government of Tamilnadu and the changing the school books is the one among them. It is also a good practice to refer the samacheer kalvi school books for TNPSC exams.


The Plus Two New Books will be followed from the academic year 2024- 2025 and all the +2 New Books will be distributed to throughout Tamilnadu. The twelfth class students can also download the 12th subject wise new text books online in the form of pdf file document. It is expected that the TN 12th std new books are better the CBSE and other state board school books with the improved practical oriented lessons.
